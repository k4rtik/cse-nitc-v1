Profile

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was started in June 1997 after trifurcating from the Department of Electrical Engineering. The department offers both post graduate and undergraduate programmes in Computer Science & Engineering, MCA programme and regular and part time doctoral degree programmes.

The fast changing scenario in the Information Technology necessitates the department to actively extend its research and development activities. The current research focus is on areas like Image Processing, Mobile Networks, Information Security, Compilers, Computer Architecture, Distributed Systems, Computational Intelligence and Multi Agent Systems.

The department has five separate research labs, common laboratories with state of the art computing facilities and a department library with reference books and other research materials. Being a forerunner in the field, the department is constantly modernizing its facilities to adapt itself to the rapidly changing technological scenario.
